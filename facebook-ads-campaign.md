# MediVac One - Facebook Advertising Campaign

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** MediVac One - Virtual Hospital Launch  
**Platform:** Facebook & Instagram (Meta)  
**Duration:** 30 days (Phase 1)  
**Budget:** $5,000 AUD  
**Target Audience:** Healthcare professionals, patients, institutions  
**Goal:** App installs, user registration, trial signups  

---

## Campaign Strategy

### Primary Objectives
1. **App Installs** - Drive downloads on Google Play
2. **Trial Signups** - Convert users to 10-day free trial
3. **Brand Awareness** - Build MediVac One recognition
4. **Engagement** - Drive in-app activity and feature exploration

### Target Audience Segments

#### Segment 1: Healthcare Professionals
- **Age:** 25-65
- **Location:** Australia, New Zealand, US, UK, Canada
- **Interests:** Healthcare, medical technology, hospital management, patient care
- **Job Titles:** Doctors, nurses, healthcare administrators, hospital staff
- **Estimated Size:** 2.5M users
- **Budget Allocation:** 40% ($2,000)

#### Segment 2: Patients & Families
- **Age:** 18-75
- **Location:** Australia, New Zealand, US, UK, Canada
- **Interests:** Health, wellness, medical services, family care
- **Estimated Size:** 8M users
- **Budget Allocation:** 35% ($1,750)

#### Segment 3: Healthcare Institutions
- **Age:** 25-65
- **Location:** Australia, New Zealand, US, UK, Canada
- **Interests:** Healthcare management, hospital technology, digital transformation
- **Job Titles:** Hospital administrators, IT directors, procurement managers
- **Estimated Size:** 500K users
- **Budget Allocation:** 25% ($1,250)

## Ad Scheduling

### Phase 1: Launch Phase (Days 1-10)
- **Focus:** App installs and brand awareness
- **Audience:** Broad healthcare interests
- **Budget:** 40% ($2,000)
- **Frequency:** 2-3 impressions per user per day

### Phase 2: Engagement Phase (Days 11-20)
- **Focus:** Trial signups and engagement
- **Audience:** Website visitors and app installers
- **Budget:** 35% ($1,750)
- **Frequency:** 3-5 impressions per user per day

### Phase 3: Conversion Phase (Days 21-30)
- **Focus:** Premium conversions and enterprise sales
- **Audience:** High-intent users and engaged users
- **Budget:** 25% ($1,250)
- **Frequency:** 2-4 impressions per user per day

## Audience Exclusions

- Users who completed trial signup
- Users who converted to paid
- Users with low engagement history
