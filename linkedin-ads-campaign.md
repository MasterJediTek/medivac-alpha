## Campaign Strategy

### Primary Objectives

The LinkedIn campaign focuses on B2B enterprise sales and institutional adoption. Key objectives include generating qualified leads from healthcare institutions, establishing thought leadership in virtual healthcare, building partnerships with hospital networks, and driving enterprise trial signups with automatic approval on $30,000+ payments.
